How to Play:
Move:		
		select Position 1 (Source)
		select Position 2 (Target)
Delete Items:		
		select Position 1 (Source)
		select the Shredder (on the Right)
Add Mission:
		select The Butten Left to your Missions
Delete Mission:	
		select The Butten Right to your Missions
Store items:
		select your Mission (Source)
		select your target position
extract items:
		select your Item (From the Shelf)
		select your Mission
